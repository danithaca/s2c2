Add Babysitters

Servuno will contact your parent friends added in the previous step for help babysit on the basis of reciprocity

Post a Paid Job

Servuno will contact your babysitters added in the previous step, as well as your friends babysitters. No babysitter can sign up on the site unless invited

Pick a Family

From those who responded yes, pick one to babysit for you

Hire a Babysitter
