Help

How does it work?

  1. Add trusted people to your personal circles and join trusted public circles.
  2. Submit a babysitter request to the circles.
  3. Pick a babysitter from those who agreed to help.
  4. Return the favor by babysitting for them.

After signed up, you will receive emails when someone could help you babysit or they need your help.

How to find a babysitter?

On your calendar page, navigate to the particular date and drag a time period you need help with. Then specify the details and press the "Submit" button. Or click here to submit a request directly.

What happens when I submit a babysitter request?

We will use the Servuno SmartMatch algorithm to find the best candidates from your personal circles, the public circles you joined, and the personal circles of people you trusted. Those candidates will receive email notifications to help you. We will also "nudge" the people you have helped before to return your favor.

Comparison to Care.com?

First, Servuno.com focuses on finding short-term babysitters for special occasions, whereas Care.com is to find long-term caregivers to help on a regular basis. Second, Servuno.com is free (well, you still need to pay the babysitter), whereas Care.com you have to pay before you find a babysitter.

Can I trust the babysitters?

Servuno.com is a convenient tool to help you find babysitters from YOUR social circles and from circles of people you trust. Ultimately you are the one responsible to make sure the chosen babysitters are trustworthy.
